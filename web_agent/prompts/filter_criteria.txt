Acceptance criteria
Include macroeconomic indicators such as:
    GDP
    Interest rates
    Exchange rates
    Inflation 

Provide real estate market data, including:
    Sale and rental prices (current and historical)
    Market trends
    Property ownership data
    Rental rates

Present demographic information:
    Population
    Income levels
    Cost of living
    Languages

Detail the real estate industry:
    Real estate and letting agencies
    Top corporations/agents
    Property listings

Include infrastructure and services data:
    Electricity prices
    Fuel costs
    Traffic data

Provide news and analysis:
    Up-to-date real estate and economic news (local and international)

Outline regulations and risk factors:
    Land ownership laws
    Foreign investment regulations
    Investment risk reports
